IAEA Travel Support:

The IAEA Ocean Acidification International Coordination Centre (OA-ICC): Travel grants are available for approximately 20 scientists from developing IAEA Member States who are working on ocean acidification. Only applicants with an accepted abstract for an oral or poster presentation will be considered for IAEA funding. Applications must be received by the IAEA not later than 25 April 2022.The information on how to apply for IAEA support for the High-CO2 Symposium is available here:

To apply for all other travel support, please indicate your need when submitting an abstract using the .doc template. Please note, researchers from developing countries and early career researchers will be prioritized.
